ABOUT US

                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               

Bidriware is a metal handicraft that originated in Bidar, Karnataka, in the 14th century. The craft involves the inlaying of silver or gold into blackened alloy of zinc and copper to create intricate designs.Bidriware, also known as Bidri craft, is a traditional metalwork art form originating from the town of Bidar in North Karnataka, India. Bidriware involves intricate craftsmanship on metal, typically using an alloy of zinc and copper. The process begins by casting the base metal into various shapes, such as vases, trays, or jewelry.
